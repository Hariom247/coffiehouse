About Us

At Coffi House in Berndorf, Germany, we pride ourselves on being a go-to destination for coffee lovers and conversation seekers alike. We're dedicated to providing an exceptional coffee experience in a cozy and inviting atmosphere, where guests can relax, unwind, and enjoy their time in comfort.

Every cup we serve is a testament to our passion for the craft — from ethically sourced beans to our expert baristas who transform them into liquid art.
